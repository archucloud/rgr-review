# RGR Review Console (encrypted)

This site hosts the Renzo Gracie Rockland curriculum review console as an **AES-256-encrypted page**. The curriculum content is not readable here; it decrypts only in your browser after you enter the review passphrase provided by Victor Erosa or Prof. Omar A. Jimenez.

Open the console: https://archucloud.github.io/rgr-review/

Reviewers: enter your name and role, work through the artifacts, then submit feedback via the Export screen (GitHub Issue buttons, or download your feedback bundle and send it to Victor). Your progress saves in your browser.
